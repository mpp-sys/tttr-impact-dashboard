Who We Are

Build What Matters. Scale What Works.

Changemakers around the world already have social solutions that work. They just need the technology to reach everyone who needs them.

Since 2019, we have worked with over 2,000 social impact organisations and mobilised a global network of 1,800+ tech companies to contribute their expertise in service of social good. Our programmes — AI Impact Lab and AI Impact Scaling Program — equip organisations with AI strategy, pro-bono tech partnerships, and end-to-end implementation support to multiply their impact while using AI responsibly.

We are one of the largest tech-for-good communities building the ecosystem for AI-powered social impact. The result: proven social interventions that reach more people, faster.
