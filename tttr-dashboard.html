Who We Are

Build What Matters. Scale What Works.

Changemakers around the world already have social solutions that work. They just need the technology to reach everyone who needs them.

Since 2019, we have worked with over 2,000 social impact organisations and mobilised a global network of 1,800+ tech companies to contribute their expertise in service of social good. Our programmes — AI Impact Lab and AI Impact Scaling Program — equip organisations with AI strategy, pro-bono tech partnerships, and end-to-end implementation support to multiply their impact while using AI responsibly.

We are one of the largest tech-for-good communities building the ecosystem for AI-powered social impact. The result: proven social interventions that reach more people, faster.

● Live

Humanitarian OpenStreetMap Team
Disaster Response · Global
● Live

fAIr is open-source AI mapping that lets local communities train their own models to detect buildings and roads from drone imagery — critical for disaster preparedness and rapid humanitarian response in areas where commercial mapping doesn't reach.

WeRobotics — Brazil Flying Labs
Climate & Disaster Response · Brazil
● Recently deployed

An AI wildfire damage classifier that ingests drone and satellite imagery to automatically map fire damage severity in Brazilian conservation units — replacing weeks of manual image analysis with near-instant, high-precision assessments for conservation managers.

Enabling faster resource allocation and safer, data-driven recovery efforts in critical biodiversity hotspots.
