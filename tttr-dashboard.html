Our Story

We believe technology can multiply social impact — if the right people connect.

Nonprofits carry the mission. Tech companies hold the tools. But these two worlds rarely meet — and when they do, it's often by accident. TTTR exists to make that connection deliberate, repeatable, and scalable.

Since 2019, we've been building the infrastructure layer for tech-powered social change: matching rigorous nonprofits with skilled tech volunteers, facilitating pro-bono collaborations, and measuring real-world outcomes. Today that infrastructure spans dozens of countries and has delivered millions in pro-bono tech value.

Now, AI is the defining inflection point. TTTR is building AI readiness for the social sector — helping nonprofits that would otherwise be left behind become confident, capable adopters of AI for good.

"We are not a marketplace. We are the infrastructure for tech-powered social change."

AI Impact Scaling Program

From AI-ready to AI-powered

For nonprofits that have completed initial AI adoption, the AI Impact Scaling Program provides long-term enablement — helping organisations build sustainable internal AI capabilities, data infrastructure, and governance frameworks.

Participants receive access to a community of practice, dedicated mentorship from senior tech volunteers, and quarterly impact reviews.
