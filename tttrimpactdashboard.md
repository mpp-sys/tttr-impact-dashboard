# TTTR Impact Dashboard — Requirements & Scope

---

## 🧭 1. Purpose & Audience

This will shape everything.

**What is the primary goal of the dashboard?**
- To showcase our global reach and impact, and be evidence for donors and other ecosystem players that we are a dynamic, impact organisation with a global real
- Other goals is to attract tech companies and motivate nonprofits to join the ecosystem
- Internally, we can also use this impact dashboard for movement growth checks

**Who is the main audience?**
- Funders (AWS, Google, etc.)
- General public
- Internal team

**What should they feel or do after seeing it?**
- Funders — Fund programs, because they trust our work and credibility
- Ecosystem players — nonprofits and tech companies — join tttr
- They should feel like they discovered or are part of the world's largest tech for good ecosystem, with a focus on AI enablement

---

## 📊 2. Core Metrics (What MUST be visible)

Think "headline impact" — not everything, just what matters.

**The 5–10 most important numbers to showcase daily:**
- Total value of delivered tech (USD)
- Number of nonprofits supported
- Number of tech companies engaged
- Number of pro bono / mission-driven collaborations
- Countries reached

**Others:**
- Active projects right now
- AI projects specifically (AI for Changemakers?)
- Lives impacted (needs to be checked)
- Number of organisations engaged in our two flagship projects:
  - AI for Changemakers
  - AI Impact Scaling Program
